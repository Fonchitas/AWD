LCA, or La Casa Amarilla, is a music and art studio that values the beauty of the creative process above the final outcome. It emphasises art for its own sake and embraces a homemade, authentic approach. The studio's aesthetic is characterised by a warm, handcrafted feel, a sense of closeness among people, and a strong presence of the colour yellow as its signature element.

PHOTOGRAPHY

The photographic style essence lies in the frontal flash, the composition of settings, the wardrobe, the color and the thematic thread behind the photo that ties the entire portfolio together. All sesions have a theme in which it sums the meaning of it. Developing a story throughout the creation of a song.

SOCIAL MEDIA

For promotion purposes and the social media visuals, I used the photoshoots to create stop motion videos that defines each chapter of the magazine. Also combining the viuals with powerfull trending music that fits the aesthetic and the tempo of the images displayed. The storys have the same structure and photographic style for the studio identity to be cohesive
